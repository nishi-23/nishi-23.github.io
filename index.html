In-House Facility

AAFT, School of cinema has Advance practical lab to give hands-on experience to its students. We have our own fully equipped Chroma Studio, Film Shooting Studio, Radio Station, and Editing Lab. AAFT organizes the Global Film Festival every year through which students get to know the current scenario of Film Industry and also get a chance to meet international and national Film Actors, Producers, Directors etc during the fest.
